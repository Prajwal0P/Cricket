My Bowling My Batting

How It Started

I didn't grow up with coaching or formal nets. No academies. No leather balls. Just a tennis ball, an open space, and a fire in the belly to bowl different.

What started casually turned into something serious. I didn't just play the game—I studied it. Watched every bounce, adjusted angles, created my own deliveries.

Cricket's not just a game for me. It taught me focus, patience, and the joy of experimenting—all of which I carry into everything I create.

Bowling Style

Fast-ish Medium

Deceptive pace variations with the same action. Batters never know what's coming next.

Off-spin / Side-arm

Spinners disguised as medium pace. The ultimate surprise weapon in tape ball cricket.

Psychological Edge

"Batters smile before facing me. After the first ball, they stop smiling."

Batting Philosophy

Aggressive Finisher

I'm not here to block. I'm here to maximize moments, punish loose balls, and change the tempo in just a few deliveries.

Comeback King

Got hit for 22 in an over once. Next innings? Returned the favor with the bat. I don't go quiet. I hit back.

Momentum Thief

Opposition celebrating? One boundary and they're rattled. That's how I bat.
